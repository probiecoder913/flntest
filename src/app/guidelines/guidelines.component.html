Test Guidelines

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting the test:

  • Ensure that you have a stable internet connection throughout the test duration.
  • Do not refresh or navigate away from the test page while taking the test.
  • Answer all the questions to the best of your knowledge.
  • For multiple-choice questions, select the most appropriate option by clicking on the radio button.
  • For subjective questions, provide your answers in the text box provided. Be clear and concise in your responses.
  • Review your answers before submitting the test. Once submitted, you will not be able to make any changes.
  • The test has a time limit of 60 minutes. The timer will be displayed on the screen.
  • Once the time is up, the test will automatically be submitted.
